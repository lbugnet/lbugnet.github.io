About

I am an Astrophysicist, specialized in stellar physics. I use Asteroseismology to unveil the dynamical processes taking place inside the core of stars like the Sun. I am currently a Flatiron Research Fellow at the Center for Computational Astrophysics of the Flatiron Insititute in New York.
